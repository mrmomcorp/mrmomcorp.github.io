Summer’s biggest event!
The HSBC World Rugby Sevens Series is coming to Cape Town and kicking off at the exhilarating Cape Town Stadium.
 
Sixteen of the world’s best Sevens Rugby teams, including the Blitz Bokke will take part in this year’s event.
 
Join us as the fans get loud, the costumes get wacky and the atmosphere get exuberant.

  DATE: 8 to 9 December 2018
  TIME: 10am onwards Saturday and Sunday
